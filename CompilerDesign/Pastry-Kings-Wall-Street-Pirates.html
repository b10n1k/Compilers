 If you are a fan of Indy film like me~be sure to catch thesetrue ‘must sees.’  In ‘The Pastry Kings’ filmmakers D A Pennebaker and Chris Hegedus follow ‘Hurt Locker’ style drama between sixteen Olympian artisans gathered in Lyon for three grueling days. The competition’s winner receives the prestigious Meilleurs Ouvriers de France direct from President Sarkozy. This blue, white and red collar medal represents the apex for French pastry chefs that cantranslate into fame and wealth.“The tension is almost unbearable.” -The SydneyMorning Herald. “I never saw so many strong men sobbing at once.”-The Guardian “…Last moments wereas thrilling as any Olympic final…heart-stopping…exquisite.”~The Independent. Have your après-film gateau pre-plated for action!
'Inside Job' 
is Oscar-nominated director Charles Ferguson‘s shocking expose that takes you inside theworld’s 2008 financial Armageddon. Your blood will boil watching this lucid analysis that reveals the arrogant,impulsive risk-takers that gamed the system, caused economic meltdown, and pulled off an insider’s $20trillion heist. These cocaine-taking, prostitution-addicted rogues have instituted a criminal racket that hascorrupted politics, academia, and reasonable government
